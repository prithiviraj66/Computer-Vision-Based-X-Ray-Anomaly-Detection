Unsupervised Reconstruction

The model learns structural distributions of normal bone scans. Abnormalities fail to reconstruct, revealing anomalies.

1

Normal Domain Training

Self-Attention Generative Adversarial Network (SAGAN) is trained strictly on non-fractured normal images. It masters the latent distribution of healthy skeletal geometry.

2

Autoencoder Compression & Synthesis

The encoder maps target hand X-rays to latent vectors, and the generator attempts to reconstruct them. Healthy structures are synthesized perfectly.

3

Pixel-Wise Error Map Estimation

Calculating absolute difference: Error(x,y) = |Input(x,y) - Reconstructed(x,y)|. Extreme discrepancies light up as hot-spots on the color-mapped image.
